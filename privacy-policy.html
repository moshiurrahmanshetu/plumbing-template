Privacy Policy

Updated: January 2026. Discover how PlumbMaster collects, encrypts, dispatches, and protects customer accounts and service locations.

Home Privacy Policy

Policy Chapters

1. Introduction 2. Data We Collect 3. Information Usage 4. Cookie Guidelines 5. Third-Party Share 6. Data Security 7. Support Inquiries

1. Introduction

Welcome to PlumbMaster. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at privacy@plumbmaster.com.

When you visit our website index.html, fill out our quote requests, or purchase equipment through our digital shop terminal, you trust us with your personal details. We take this trust with maximum corporate seriousness and enforce rigid data protection measures.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering, booking dispatches, or filling out forms. This data includes:

  • Contact Parameters: First and last names, email addresses, phone coordinates, and billing/shipping addresses.
  • Service Coordinates: Structural blueprints, pipe layouts, water line photos, and address maps required for mechanical dispatch.
  • Credentials: Passwords, security hints, and similar authentication data used to access your PlumbMaster customer locker.
  • Financial Data: Secure payment tokens, billing details, card parameters, and invoicing transactions. All processing is proxied securely.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for purposes based on legitimate business interests, the fulfillment of our service contract with you, compliance with our legal obligations, and/or your explicit consent. Specifically, we use collected parameters to:

  • Facilitate user registration, account creation, and coordinate secure technician login keys.
  • Dispatch emergency mechanical teams to verified structural locations in real time.
  • Deliver tracking links with technician GPS coordinates and profile photographs.
  • Process payments, manage purchases, and deliver itemized tax and invoice receipts.
  • Distribute promotional coupons, product newsletters, and seasonal plumbing checklists.

4. Cookie & Tracking Policy

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to access or store information. Cookies help us analyze web traffic flow, remember user cart selections, and store light/dark mode theme preferences securely.

You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, this may prevent you from taking full advantage of the PlumbMaster store and billing features.

5. Third-Party Data Share

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations. We share data only with the following key partner sectors:

  • Billing & Gateway Platforms: To authorize card charges and clear secure escrow payments.
  • GPS & Mapping Services: To deliver real-time vehicle dispatch maps and distance calculations.
  • SMS & Email Gateways: To broadcast automated booking alerts and dispatch tracking links.
  • Legal Entities: When strictly subpoenaed under federal mechanics, code, or safety auditing investigations.

6. Data Encryption & Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. All connections use Transport Layer Security (TLS/SSL) encryption, and client profiles are protected inside isolated, sandboxed server nodes.

However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our website is at your own risk. You should only access the services within a secure environment.

7. Privacy Support & Contacts

If you have questions or comments about this policy, or want to request a full audit, correction, or permanent removal of your personal parameters, please reach out to our legal officer:
