2. Contracting & Field Scope

PlumbMaster provides premium mechanical plumbing solutions, pipeline replacements, high-pressure jetting, leak diagnostics, and luxury fixture assemblies. When engaging our crew for on-site services, the following terms apply:

  • Site Access: The client must guarantee clear, safe, and unobstructed access to all water main shutoffs, utility corridors, crawled spaces, and mechanical shafts.
  • Structural Approvals: It is the client's absolute responsibility to obtain any specific homeowner association (HOA) clearances or localized building permits unless explicitly requested inside our itemized quotation.
  • Diagnostic Reports: We formulate recommended repair paths based on comprehensive high-resolution camera diagnostics. If the client declines proceeding with recommended repairs, our standard trip fee remains applicable.

3. Emergency Dispatches

Our emergency crews operate 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. However, emergency bookings have strict dispatch parameters:

  • Estimated Arrival Time (ETA): Our ETAs are calculated using live road transit models. While we average under 45 minutes, severe weather events or transport gridlocks are beyond our mechanical control and do not constitute contract violations.
  • Emergency Premium: After-hours and holiday dispatches have minor standard adjustments which are always state-verified with dispatchers over phone logs.
  • Damage Containment: Our emergency priority is immediate water containment to safeguard property. Complete mechanical reconstruction of burst lines might require secondary scheduling under daylight shifts.

4. Store Purchases & Material Warranty

When purchasing plumbing components, luxury faucets, or fittings through our digital shop terminal, the following protocols apply:

  • Product Specifications: We source original equipment from elite manufacturers. Check matching sizes and compliance requirements before placing order cards.
  • Return Period: Unused, uninstalled products returned inside original packaging are eligible for refunds within 30 days of shipment, subject to minor restocking fees.
  • Manufacturer Warranty: Installed appliances (water heaters, filtration setups, sump pumps) are backed by direct factory product warranties, which we register on your behalf.

5. Invoicing & Payments

Payments for on-site diagnostics, parts procurement, and mechanical labor follow strict schedules:

  • Written Estimates: All tasks require signed digital authorizations before starting. Authorizations lock in pricing structures.
  • Payment Due: Residential service invoices are due immediately upon task completion. Commercial accounts may register Net-30 payment arrangements, subject to credit score verification.
  • Unpaid Balances: Delinquent accounts past Net-30 are subject to standard state interest penalties of 1.5% per month, plus any associated collections agency fees.
