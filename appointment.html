Booking & Scheduling Help

Get detailed answers regarding scheduling, rescheduling, and preparing for our engineers.

Our smart automated dispatcher instantly logs your location parameters. You will receive an SMS confirmation within 15 minutes, followed by a voice call from your chosen technician to verify your entry gate and pressure dynamics.

The online estimations represent a reliable engineering baseline. Once our lead technician arrives at your site and tests physical pressure and acoustic signals, they will verify the flat rate before commencing any work.

You can reschedule or cancel up to 2 hours before the dispatch slot with zero penalties. Simply click the unique reservation link in your SMS confirmation or call our hotline desk.
