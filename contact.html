Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers regarding plumbing diagnostics, emergency dispatches, pricing plans, and warranties.

We define plumbing emergencies as issues that cause active structural damage, present sanitary hazards, or disrupt core water supplies. This includes actively burst water pipes, sewer main backups, failed water boilers or heaters in freezing temperatures, major water supply line blockages, or active interior flooding. Our emergency lines are mobilized immediately for these cases.

Our average physical response time in metropolitan areas is 30 to 45 minutes from the moment you confirm your address with our dispatcher. We have service trucks parked strategically across different neighborhoods to minimize transport latency and guarantee immediate on-site support.

We pride ourselves on transparent, honest rates. Standard residential and commercial maintenance are billed at our standard rate schedule. For extreme emergency dispatches requested outside of regular business hours (10:00 PM to 6:00 AM) or on major public holidays, a transparent, pre-disclosed emergency dispatch fee is applied.

Our operations are protected by a comprehensive $5,000,000 liability insurance policy and general bonding.
