Senior Plumber Specification

Review core performance indicators, structural prerequisites, and benefits packages for this specific field or administrative role.

Home Careers Job Details
Compensation $85k - $110k / yr
Location Metro Hub HQ
Prerequisite 5+ Years Exp

Job Description

We are seeking an elite, certified Senior Plumber to direct high-end residential refittings, commercial soldering, and master pipeline layouts. You will lead on-site teams, analyze structural architectural blueprints, and enforce rigid municipal safety and plumbing codes.

Working Hours & Schedules

Monday - Friday (8:00 AM - 5:00 PM), on-call rotation once a month

Core Responsibilities
  • Analyze complex technical plumbing diagrams and structural blueprints.
  • Solder copper water lines, assemble iron gas piping, and join high-density polyethylene pipelines.
  • Oversee on-site crews, distributing daily task cards and conducting safety checks.
  • Coordinate sewer camera scans, diagnostics, and high-pressure jetting schedules.
  • Engage with elite commercial building owners, verifying service goals and filing completion documents.
Candidate Requirements
  • Active Master Plumber state licensing credential with zero active penalties.
  • Minimum of 5 years of verifiable field experience on commercial or luxury residential projects.
  • Expert knowledge of mechanical piping codes and standard OSHA safety parameters.
  • Excellent leadership, communication, and client correspondence skills.
  • Valid driving license with a clean historical record.
Compensation & Benefits Perks
  • Competitive Salary with performance incentives.
  • 100% employer-covered health, visual, and dental insurance plans.
  • Brand-new, fully custom Mercedes service rig.
  • 6% 401(k) company match with immediate vesting.
  • Generous paid-time-off and holiday benefits.
