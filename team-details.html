Professional Bio

The Journey to Chief Architecture

Marcus Vance is a legendary figure in the municipal fluid systems community. Over his prestigious 18-year tenure, Marcus has directed the structural engineering setups for some of the state's most complex high-rise residential projects and heavy industrial fluid arrays.

Combining deep theoretical hydraulics expertise with a robust, hands-on master plumber pedigree, he specializes in high-velocity backflow dynamics, variable pressure thermal regulators, and acoustic pipeline dampening. He is regularly summoned by city planning boards to run compliance audits and troubleshoot structural system vibration noise.

"Plumbing isn't about applying tape to a failing joint—it's about engineering perfect static systems that behave with absolute predictability for fifty years."
